<objective>
Consolidate the four near-identical CLI adapters (`copilot_cli.py`, `cursor_cli.py`, `claude_code_cli.py`, `gemini_cli.py`) into ONE concrete `CliEngineAdapter` driven by a declarative `CliEngineSpec` (D-01). The registry auto-populates by iterating `CLI_ENGINE_SPECS` (no manual import+dict edit per engine). `SKELETON_ENGINES` collapses to a `functional` spec field (D-03), and the Gemini skeleton is replaced by a functional Antigravity spec entry (D-12). This is ENGN-10 — it MUST land FIRST so PERF-03/ENGN-08/ENGN-09 are added once against the generic, not 4× against copy-pasted adapters.

Purpose: Make adding a CLI engine ~1 data entry and make every later contract change a single edit on the generic.
Output: `spec.py`, `cli_adapter.py`, rewritten `registry.py`, slimmed per-engine modules keeping their `AuthError` subclasses + `__all__`, replaced gemini→antigravity spec, updated contract + registry tests.
</objective>

<objective>
Enforce the dismiss suppress-list at gate-assembly time with the two
invalidation guards that keep a dismissal from becoming a place real findings go
to die: guard 2 (auto-expire when the code at the dismissed location changes)
and guard 3 (override when the engine re-emits at a higher severity). An active
dismissal drops its finding from the gate input while staying visible in the
sticky audit (D-15).

Purpose: This is the load-bearing safety logic of the dismiss model. It is pure
functions over the open set reusing shipped predicates (`finding_region_changed`
C=3, `SEVERITY_RANK`), wired at the `_open_set_findings` → `apply_gate` seam
(Landmine L3 — compose at the existing subtraction point, do not duplicate the
union). Creation of entries comes later (08-15) behind the authorized command;
this plan can be tested end-to-end with a hand-built sticky dismiss-block fixture.
Output: `active_suppressed_fingerprints` in `dismiss.py` + its wiring in
`run_review`.
</objective>
